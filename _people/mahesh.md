### Bio:

<p style="text-align: justify">
Gulla Mahesh is an M.Tech(Research Assistant) student in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Hyderabad. Prior to joining IITH, he completed his B.Tech from JNTUA College Of Engineering Ananthapuramu. Before Joining IITH, he worked on projects in Medical image classification and medical speech to text. Currently he is part of the Natural Language and Information Processing Lab where he works on interpretability of outputs generated by Large Language Models (LLMs).
